How Smart Tech Can Save The World

Smart tech is important in our current world. Through innovation, we can help change the issues in the world via this technology.

Farming

Smart technology is very important to farming. For example, smart techn can be used to remotely monitor the weather, soil composition as well as monitor cattle for whether or not they are close to being in labour.

Medicine

In medicine, we use smart technology to monitor patients, provide surgery remotely and santize surfaces.

Construction

Smart tech is used in construction to precisely cut material, increase health and saftey and streamline efficency

Foresting

Foresting teams use smart tech for such applications as monitoring tree health.
