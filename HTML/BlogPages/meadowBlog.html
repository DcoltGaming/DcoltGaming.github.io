Why sustanability is important in business

Vodafone consider sustanability within everything that they do. They work on the primary four pillars of sustainability:

Digital Society

Vodafone connect people and business to our digital society. Through the use of Gigabit networks, IoT and mobile financial services we enable innovation and technologies to be developed to help make our lves easier, healther, smarter and more fulfilling.

Acting responsibly

Vodafone are determined to act responsibly and with integrity in what ever country they operate. They do what matters but also how they work matters. They strive to ensure they always act lawfully, ethically and with integrity wherever they operate.

Climate Change

Vodafone believe that urgent and substained action is required to address climate change and that business success should not come as a cost to the enviroment. They have committed to halving their enviromental impact by 2025.

Reducing Emmissions

Vodafone's are determined to help their customers to become more efficient and in turn reduce their emissions. Their technologies and services provid their customers with the means to achieve a reduction in their greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions. They are commited to helping all business customers reduce their own carbon emissions by a cumulative total of 350 million tonnes globally by 2030.

Inclusion For All

Vodafone supports inclusion for all. Opportunities and the promise of a better digital future should be accessible for all. Through our techology we will work to bridge the divides that exist and help people to conribute equally and fully to society.
